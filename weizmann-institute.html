Role Overview

As Projects Director within the Construction Division at the world-renowned Weizmann Institute of Science, I led a dedicated section responsible for the planning, design, construction, and commissioning of complex projects for research facilities. My work included managing advanced laboratory spaces,implementing seismic retrofitting for heritage buildings, and overseeing infrastructure upgrades across the campus. I directed all section activities and budgets, ensuring alignment with institutional goals while consistently delivering projects on time and within budget.
