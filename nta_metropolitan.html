Role Overview

The Red Line LRT represents a historic achievement as Israel's first underground light rail system. As Senior Project Manager, I served as the owner's representative for this $7 billion CAD project, managing $450M responsibility for three underground stations. This role required coordinating the most complex urban infrastructure project in Israeli history, navigating dense urban environments, managing 24/6 operations, and integrating cutting-edge transit technology while ensuring seamless coordination between multiple contractors and stakeholders across five municipalities in Tel Aviv's metropolitan area.
